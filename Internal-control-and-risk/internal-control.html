The “types of control” section is covered at a proficiency level. All the other areas are covered at an awareness level, which means you only need to have a working knowledge of the terminology and fundamentals. 

This Section focuses on controls and the processes that help organizations achieve their goals and objectives. Without a reliable control system, the planning process is much less valuable and less useful to the organization. The IAA assists the organization in maintaining effective controls. 

A tight control process contributes to the organization’s overall improvement in the following areas:  

  • Reliability and integrity of financial and operating information  
  • Efficiency and effectiveness of operations 
  • Safeguarding of assets 
  • Compliance with laws, regulations, and contracts
