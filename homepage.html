CIA Part 1 Exam Overview

The CIA Part 1 exam, Internal Audit Basics, is 150 minutes long and consists of 125 multiple-choice questions. This exam tests mandatory guidance from the International Professional Practices Framework (IPPF), internal control and risk concepts, and tools and techniques for conducting internal audit engagements.

For more information: www.iia.org

Exam Sections

Section I - Mandatory Guidance

35-45%

Section II - Internal Control and Risk

25-35%

Section III - Conducting Internal Audit Engagements

25-35%

Knowledge Levels

Sections I & III are tested at the Proficiency Level (thorough understanding). Section II is tested at the Awareness Level (basic knowledge).

Study Strategy

Use the textbook, study bank, and practice exams to understand the depth and manner in which topics are tested.
